Meet Our Star Performers

Prepare to be enchanted by the soulful voices of Gujarat's most celebrated artists, live on stage!

Day 1: The Queen of Dandiya

Falguni Pathak

The undisputed queen of Garba, Falguni Pathak, will kickstart the festival with her iconic voice and chart-topping hits. Get ready to dance the night away to classics that have defined Navratri for generations.

Day 2: The Folk Maestro

Kirtidan Gadhvi

Experience the powerful and rustic voice of Kirtidan Gadhvi. Known for his energetic stage presence and mastery of folk music, he will bring an authentic and electrifying atmosphere to the second night of our festival.

Day 3: The Digital Sensation

Kinjal Dave

Closing the festival with a bang is the youth icon, Kinjal Dave. Her peppy numbers and modern take on folk music are guaranteed to have you on your feet for a grand finale you won't forget.
